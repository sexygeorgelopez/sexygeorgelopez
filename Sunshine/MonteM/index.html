MonteM

MonteM are Male Piantas. There are eight unique types of Male Pianta in Super Mario Sunshine, and the
type is determined by the letter value after MonteM (A-H). Each type of Pianta requires its own unique files
in the level that it exists in, and that includes a unique manager. The type determines the main piece of
clothing the Pianta is wearing, which is permanently attached to the model.

  • MonteMA - Piantas who are wearing a vest.
  • MonteMB - Piantas who are wearing a vest with an undershirt.
  • MonteMC - Piantas who are wearing a uniform / V-Neck.
  • MonteMD - Piantas who are wearing a sleeveless shirt.
  • MonteME - Piantas who are stuck in a cannon.
  • MonteMF - Ducky Float Piantas.
  • MonteMG - Piantas who are sweeping with a broom.
  • MonteMH - Piantas who are playing a ukelele. (Same as MonteMA)

Each Pianta contains multiple unique parameters that can be edited in a level's scene.bin. 
Assuming you're using the most recent parameters created for Sunshine, these values are:

  • BodyColor - The body color. These are predetermined.
  • ClothesColor - The color of the clothes that are attached to the model.
  • Acc1Color - Determines the color of the Hat, as well as the Bowtie.
  • Acc2Color - Determines the color of both the Mustache and the Neckerchief.
  • Acc3Color - Determines the color of glasses.
  • Accessory - Determines the combination of hat, neck accessory, glasses, and mustache.
  • Idle - The idle animation.
