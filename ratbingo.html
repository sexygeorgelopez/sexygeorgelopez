About

This is a "Bingo" board for Super Mario Sunshine races.

To win, you must complete 5 of the tasks in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

The seed number is used to generate the board. Changing the seed will make a new board.

You can click on the squares to turn them green and red. This may help you organize your route planning.

For SMS-Bingo, there are some specific rules in place:

Basic Rules:

  • Start from a new file on GO!
  • You are done when you finish the 5th objective. You do not have to defeat bowser.

Restrictions:

  • Gelato Beach skip is NOT allowed!

Individual Item Rules:

  • X Lives - Your number of lives must be at least X when the game is over.
  • X Total Blue Coins - Collect at least X Blue Coins. You are allowed to trade blue coins for shines, be sure to remember how many you spent!
  • X Blue Coins from Enemies - Enemies are things that hurt, so a blue coin from bees counts, whereas a blue coin from butterflies or birds does not.
  • Linked Blue Coin Pairs - These are the Graffiti's with X, O, or Triangles, for the goal to count you must collect both of the blue coins that are connected to each other.
  • Blue Coins from M Graffiti - Some blue coins are obtained by spraying Graffiti with the letter M, this goal is gained by collecting specifically those blue coins.
  • X Shines from Delfino Plaza - Even though they are counted on the Z screen (in game), for the purpose of bingo, shines from blue coins do not count towards this goal.
  • X Shines from X Levels - Collect the necessary amount of shines as listed by the goal. Example - 5 Shines from 2 Levels, that means collect at least 5 shines in each of the 2 levels (= 10 shines). Note that Delfino Plaza shines do not count towards collecting shines from levels.
  • X Red Coin Shines - It doesn't matter if they are on a timer (appear after ground pounding a red button) or not.
  • X Secret Shines - These are the episodes with "secret" in the name, where FLUDD gets taken away after reaching the secret area (doesn't include The Goopy Inferno). If you collect the red coins after 1st completion, it won't count twice towards this goal.
  • X Hidden Shines - These are the shines that are listed under "???" on the score screen, every stage has 2 hidden shines.
  • X Boss Shines - Some bosses (Petey Piranha and Gooper Blooper) have rematches, if the item requires unique bosses, these rematches don't count. (but the first fight counts!) You have to get the death animation of the boss, if you skip the boss and just collect the shine, it doesn't count (Noki2).
  • X Shadow Marios - Complete episode 7 of X stages, Delfino Plaza doesn't count.
  • A list of all shines and whether of not they meet certain requirements can be found here.
